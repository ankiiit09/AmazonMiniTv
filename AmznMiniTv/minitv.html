How to access Amazon miniTV?

miniTV content is a free, ad-supported video streaming service available in India only. miniTV content is accessible on iOS 14 and above, Android OS version 6 and above, Fire TV devices updated to the latest software version, and on all major web browsers. Accessing Amazon miniTV online is a breeze. Download the Amazon shopping application from the iOS or Android app store, open the app and click on miniTV icon on the top right side of the homepage.
